# AudioNote AI: Feature Roadmap

Based on the analysis of leaders in the space (like AudioNotes.app), here is the proposed roadmap for evolving your application.

---

## 🚀 Phase 1: Enhanced Capture (Short Term)
*Goal: Remove friction from getting data into the app.*

*   **[ ] YouTube URL Input**: Allow users to paste a link and have the AI transcribe and summarize the video.
*   **[ ] Document/Image OCR**: Use OCR to scan physical handouts or PDFs and combine them with your audio notes.
*   **[ ] File Upload Support**: Support for `.mp3`, `.wav`, `.m4a`, and `.mp4` file uploads from the device storage.

## 🧠 Phase 2: Technical Excellence (Core Processing)
*Goal: Improve the quality and depth of the generated notes.*

*   **[ ] Speaker Identification (Diarization)**: Distinguish between the Lecturer and Students during a recording.
*   **[ ] 80+ Multi-language Support**: Expand beyond standard languages; refine Hausa/Yoruba/etc. accuracy.
*   **[ ] Key Action Item Extraction**: Automatically generate a "To-Do" list at the end of each summary.
*   **[ ] Note Formatting**: Support for **Markdown** rendering in the app (headers, bold text, lists).

## 💬 Phase 3: Interactive Learning (The "Killer" Feature)
*Goal: Transform notes from static text into an active study buddy.*

*   **[ ] AI Chat with Note**: Allow users to ask questions like: *"What did the professor say would be on the exam?"* or *"Explain the concept of quantum entanglement from this lecture."*
*   **[ ] Multi-Note Synthesis**: Ask the AI to compare points from Monday's lecture and Wednesday's lecture.
*   **[ ] Quiz Generation**: Automatically generate 5 multiple-choice questions based on the note.

## 📂 Phase 4: Organization & Sync (Scalability)
*Goal: Manage a growing library of academic materials.*

*   **[ ] Folders & Tagging**: Group notes by course code (e.g., "CSC101", "BIO202").
*   **[ ] Notion Direct Sync**: Push summaries directly to a designated Notion database.
*   **[ ] Web Dashboard**: Access and edit notes on a larger screen (Laptop/Desktop).
*   **[ ] WhatsApp/Slack Bot**: Send a voice note to a bot and have it appear in your app instantly.
